Our Story

Our story began in 2015 in Palo Alto, CA—Lyuba teaching a summer class and Andrew at a software internship. Thanks to some good fortune, a first date turned into a relationship that continued back in Boston, with the help of many Peter Pan bus rides between Tufts and Wellesley.

After graduating, Andrew went back to the San Francisco area for work, while Lyuba kept herself busy studying Russian and math that increasingly looked more like Greek. After two years and tens of thousands of miles in the air for weekends that always felt too short, we finally ended up in the same city again, settling into a cozy Beacon Hill apartment.

In the years since then, our life together has been filled with countless adventures, baking experiments (Andrew in the kitchen, Lyuba on quality assurance) and raising two wonderful kittens named Ben & Jerry. We wouldn't have it any other way.

It felt only right to propose on March 14, 2020 (also known as Pi Day) while walking through Boston's Public Garden. We can't wait to celebrate the start of our next big chapter together with you on March 12th, 2022.
